DATA ANALYST
PT Enseval Medika Prima

About the role

We are seeking a talented Data Analyst to join our dynamic team at PT Enseval Medika Prima. As a full-time Data Analyst, you will play a crucial role in transforming raw data into actionable insights that drive business strategy and decision-making. This position is based in East Jakarta, Jakarta.

What you'll be doing

Collecting, cleaning, and organising data from various sources
Analysing data to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities
Developing and maintaining dashboards and reports to effectively communicate insights
Collaborating with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements and translate them into data-driven solutions
Providing data-driven recommendations to improve business performance
Continuously exploring new data sources and techniques to enhance the analytical capabilities
Ensuring data integrity and security by implementing appropriate controls and processes
What we're looking for

Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field such as Mathematics, Statistics, or Computer Science
Minimum 2 years of experience in a data analyst or similar role
Proficient in data manipulation and analysis using tools such as SQL, Excel, and Power BI
Strong problem-solving and critical thinking skills
Ability to translate complex data into clear and actionable insights
Excellent communication and presentation skills to effectively convey findings
Familiarity with data visualisation techniques and best practices
Adaptability and willingness to learn new technologies and methodologies
